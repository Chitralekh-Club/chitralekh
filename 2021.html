Rules and Regulation to Competition




VIDEOGRAPHY DOMAIN

  • Only 30 Sec Video would be taken.
  • Your Own Edited Video will be accepted.
  • Template can be use.
  • Submit your video in mp4, avi, mov, mts only.
  • Submit your video with your name and suitable caption.

PHOTOGRAPHY DOMAIN

  • For Photography the theme is YOUR BEST SHOT.
  • Photo Manipulation is not allowed.
  • Some Basic Editing is allowed.
  • Participants have to send both original and Edited one
  • The Size of Photo should be greater than 3MB.
  • Submit your photo with your name and suitable caption.
  • No Watermark
  • Should be submitted only jpg, jpeg and png extension only.

WRITING DOMAIN

  • Upload only your orginal work.
  • Writing can be accepted both digital and manual.
  • For Manual Writing paper should be used only A4 Size.
  • For Digital Writing you can submit using docx, docm, dot, dotx extension only.
  • Short Story - 150 to 200 words.
  • Poetry - 80 words.
  • Quote - 30 to 40 words.

ART DOMAIN

  • Paper should be used only A3 and A4 size.
  • Accepted art shaded black and white also.
  • No Watermark or Add Mark.
  • Some filter to be used while submitting.
  • Participant have to send both original and filter one.
  • Art should be submit with your name and suitable caption.
